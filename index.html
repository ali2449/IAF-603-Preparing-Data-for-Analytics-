Course Overview

The IAF 603: Preparing Data for Analytics course is a cornerstone of the Master of Science in Informatics and Analytics (MSIA) program at the University of North Carolina at Greensboro (UNCG). This course equips students with the essential skills to manage, clean, and transform structured, semi-structured, and unstructured data for analytics. Through 14 modules, students will master SQL and Spark SQL, learning to handle real-world datasets with techniques like data cleaning, normalization, joins, and advanced querying. Designed for both beginners and those with some programming experience, this course prepares you for data-driven decision-making in fields like healthcare, business, and informatics.

Course Modules
